Ford Gobike Trip Data Exploration¶

By Satvinder Kaur¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at how the given features in Ford Gobike trip data can affect the trip durations. I also wanted to see different trends in number of trips over the period of time. My main focus was on these features: Trip duration, User type, Start Day, month and hour of a trip.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset contains information about Ford Gobike trips from 2017. Ford Gobike is now known as Bay wheels which is a regional public bicycle sharing system in San Francisco Bay Area, California. There are 519,700 rows in this dataset each representing an individual bike trip with 16 features. The features used in this analysis are as follows:

  • duration_sec - Trip duration in seconds
  • user_type - User type (Subscriber or Customer)
  • month - Month when trip started
  • day - Day of week when trip started
  • hour - Hour of a day when trip started

Distribution of Trip Durations¶

  • Trip duration values in this dataset ranges from 0 to 10000 seconds.
  • When plotted on a logarithmic scale, the distribution of Trip durations takes on unimodal shape with a peak around 600 seconds, which indicates that the average trip duration is 10 minutes.

Trends of Gobike trips taken on weekdays vs weekends¶

  • The distribution for Gobike trips on weekdays is bimodal with two peaks around 7am to 9am and 4pm to 6pm indicating that Gobike services were used for work/school commute.
  • The distribution for weekend Gobike trips is unimodal with a peak at 1pm, and most of the trips were taken in the middle of the day from 10am to 6pm.
  • Most late night trips i.e after 11pm till 3am early morning are taken on weekends.
